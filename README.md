# Orbitto
Orbitto is a SaaS platform that helps organizations discover and utilize the talent they already have. Unlike traditional freelance platforms, where gigs get boosted based on reviews from strangers, Orbitto operates within a trusted internal network where colleagues already know each other's work ethic, skills, and reliability.
Companies subscribe to Orbitto and onboard their employees, creating a private talent marketplace exclusive to their organization. Employees can post gigs, offer services, and hire fellow colleagues for internal projects, making skill-sharing seamless, trusted, and efficient.
No more hiring externally for tasks your own team can handle. No more hidden talent going unnoticed. Orbitto brings your organization's skills to the surface.
